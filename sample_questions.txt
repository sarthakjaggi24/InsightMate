Manager Role – Sample Questions:

- What is the current progress of the AI-based chatbot project?
- Which tasks are still pending in this week's sprint?
- When is the final demo of the project scheduled?
- Are there any blockers or risks reported by the team?
- How many sprint goals have we achieved so far?

Intern Role – Sample Questions:

- What are the tasks assigned to team members for this week?
- When do I need to update my progress on the dashboard?
- How many casual leaves can i take?

HR Role – Sample Questions:

- What is the maximum number of casual leaves allowed in a year?
- Can employees carry forward unused leaves?
- Is there any notice period required for long leaves?
- How is sick leave policy different from casual leave?
